1. Cloud Services and Cloud Models
  • Cloud Computing ?
    • Cloud Computing is the delivery of computing services -
including(such as) servers, storage, databases, networking, software, analytics, intelligence, and more, 
over the Cloud (Internet).
to offer faster innovation, flexible resources, and economies of scale.
  • why Cloud Computing ?
    • We can take any required services on rent. 
The cloud computing services will be charged based on usage.
  • Where is cloud computing used?
    • Cloud Computing refers to the delivery of on-demand computing services over the Internet on an as-needed basis. 
It allows businesses to rent access to computing services 
like servers, storage, databases, analytics, networking, software, and intelligence, typically over the Internet.
